## What is AI Interview Coach?

AI Interview Coach is an intelligent interview preparation platform that helps you practice for job interviews using artificial intelligence. Here's what it can do for you:

### Key Features:
- **AI-Powered Interviews**: Practice with an AI interviewer that asks relevant questions based on your resume and the job you're applying for
- **Real-time Feedback**: Get instant feedback on your responses during the interview
- **Resume Analysis**: Upload your resume and get personalized questions tailored to your experience
- **Job-Specific Questions**: Upload job descriptions to get questions that match the specific role you're targeting
- **Voice Recognition**: The system can hear and understand your spoken responses
- **Comprehensive Reports**: Receive detailed feedback on your performance, including strengths and areas for improvement

### How It Works:
1. Upload the job description for the position you're applying for
2. Upload your resume to match against the job requirements
3. The AI analyzes both documents and generates relevant interview questions
4. Review the generated questions with 3 difficulty levels and sample answers
5. Schedule your interview and complete payment
6. Attend your AI-powered interview session
7. Receive detailed feedback and suggestions for improvement

This tool is perfect for anyone preparing for job interviews, whether you're a recent graduate or an experienced professional looking to advance your career.

## How does the interview process work?

Understanding the interview process will help you get the most out of your practice session. Here's what happens during an AI Interview Coach interview:

### Interview Flow:

#### 1. **Opening and Introduction**
- The AI interviewer will greet you and introduce the session
- You'll receive an overview of what to expect
- The system will confirm your audio and video setup

#### 2. **Question Presentation**
- Questions are generated based on the job description and your resume
- Each question appears on screen with audio narration
- Questions cover different areas: technical skills, behavioral scenarios, and role-specific knowledge

#### 3. **Your Response Time**
- You'll have time to think and formulate your answer
- Speak clearly into your microphone
- The system records your response for analysis

#### 4. **Real-time Analysis**
- The AI analyzes your response for:
  - Content relevance and accuracy
  - Communication skills
  - Confidence and clarity
  - Technical knowledge demonstration

#### 5. **Follow-up Questions**
- The interviewer may ask follow-up questions based on your responses
- This creates a more natural conversation flow
- Helps assess deeper understanding of topics

#### 6. **Interview Conclusion**
- The session ends with a summary
- You'll receive immediate feedback on your performance
- Detailed analysis and recommendations are generated

### Types of Questions You Might Encounter:

#### **Behavioral Questions**
- "Tell me about a time when you had to solve a difficult problem"
- "Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member"
- "Give an example of a project you led successfully"

#### **Technical Questions**
- Role-specific technical knowledge
- Problem-solving scenarios
- Industry best practices

#### **Situational Questions**
- "What would you do if..."
- "How would you handle..."
- Scenario-based problem solving

### What the AI Evaluates:
- **Communication Skills**: Clarity, structure, and confidence
- **Technical Knowledge**: Accuracy and depth of understanding
- **Problem-Solving**: Approach to challenges and solutions
- **Cultural Fit**: Alignment with company values and role requirements

### Tips for Better Performance:
- **Use the STAR Method**: Situation, Task, Action, Result for behavioral questions
- **Be Specific**: Provide concrete examples from your experience
- **Stay Relevant**: Keep answers focused on the job requirements
- **Ask for Clarification**: If you don't understand a question, ask for more details

## What should I do if my interview gets interrupted?

Technical issues or interruptions can happen during interviews. Here's what to do if your interview gets interrupted:

### Common Types of Interruptions:

#### **Technical Issues**
- Internet connection problems
- Audio or video equipment failures
- Browser or application crashes
- Power outages

#### **Personal Interruptions**
- Phone calls or messages
- Family or work interruptions
- Environmental distractions
- Time constraints

### Immediate Steps to Take:

#### Step 1: Don't Panic
- Stay calm and assess the situation
- Most interruptions can be resolved quickly
- The system is designed to handle brief disruptions

#### Step 2: Try to Resume
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the browser page
- Restart your audio/video equipment
- Log back into your account

#### Step 3: Check Interview Status
- Go to your interview history
- See if the interview is still active
- Look for resume or restart options

### Technical Troubleshooting:

#### **Internet Connection Issues**
- Check your Wi-Fi or ethernet connection
- Try switching to a different network
- Restart your router if necessary
- Use a mobile hotspot as backup

#### **Audio/Video Problems**
- Check microphone and camera permissions
- Test your equipment in browser settings
- Try different browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Restart your computer if needed

#### **Browser Issues**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Update your browser to the latest version

### What Happens to Your Progress:

#### **Automatic Saving**
- The system saves your progress automatically
- Completed questions and responses are preserved
- You can from resume where you left off

#### **Interview Status**
- Check if your interview is marked as "In Progress"
- Look for resume options in your dashboard
- Contact support if the interview appears lost

### Resuming Your Interview:

#### **If You Can Resume**
- Log back into your account
- Go to your interview history
- Look for "Resume Interview" or similar option
- Continue from where you left off

#### **If You Need to Restart**
- Some interruptions may require starting over
- This is normal and not a problem
- You'll get fresh questions and a new experience

### Preventing Future Interruptions:

#### **Technical Preparation**
- Test your equipment before starting
- Use a stable internet connection
- Close unnecessary applications
- Have backup devices ready

#### **Environment Setup**
- Choose a quiet, private location
- Inform others not to disturb you
- Turn off notifications on your devices
- Have water and notes ready

#### **Time Management**
- Schedule interviews when you have uninterrupted time
- Allow extra time for potential issues
- Don't rush through the setup process

### When to Contact Support:

#### **Contact Support If:**
- You can't resume your interview after trying troubleshooting steps
- Your progress appears to be lost
- You experience repeated technical issues
- You need help with account or payment issues

#### **Information to Provide:**
- Your account email or username
- The time when the interruption occurred
- What type of interruption happened
- What troubleshooting steps you've already tried

### Getting Help:

#### **Self-Help Resources**
- Check the FAQ section
- Review troubleshooting guides
- Look for status updates on the website

#### **Contact Support**
- Use the contact form on the website
- Send an email to support
- Include detailed information about your issue

### Tips for Smooth Interviews:
- **Test Everything First**: Check all equipment before starting
- **Have Backup Plans**: Know what to do if things go wrong
- **Stay Calm**: Most issues can be resolved quickly
- **Document Problems**: Note what happened for support if needed

# Interview Coach

A comprehensive interview preparation platform designed to help job seekers improve their interview skills through practice, feedback, and personalized coaching.

## Features

- **Mock Interviews**: Practice with realistic interview scenarios
- **AI-Powered Feedback**: Get instant feedback on your responses and body language
- **Question Bank**: Access a vast library of common interview questions by role and industry
- **Performance Analytics**: Track your progress and identify areas for improvement
- **Personalized Coaching**: Receive tailored advice based on your performance
- **Video Recording**: Record and review your interview sessions
- **Industry-Specific Prep**: Specialized content for different job sectors
